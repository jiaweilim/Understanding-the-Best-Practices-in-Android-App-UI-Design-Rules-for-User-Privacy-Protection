Customs@SG is a latest service offering by Singapore Customs.

Travellers with purchases exceeding their personal duty free concession or Goods & Services Tax (GST) relief can use the app to declare and pay the duty and/or GST for the goods purchased, anytime anywhere prior to their arrival in Singapore.

The app also allows members of the public to feedback or send enquiries relating to Customs matters.

With Customs@SG, you can easily access the following services at your fingertips:

- Declare and pay duty and/or GST on your overseas purchases
- Provide feedback on customs matters
