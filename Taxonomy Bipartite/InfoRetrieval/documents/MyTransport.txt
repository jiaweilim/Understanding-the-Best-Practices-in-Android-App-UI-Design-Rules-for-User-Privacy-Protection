The new MyTransport.SG mobile app now offers you with more useful travelling information and new features to help you get around Singapore.

New features of MyTransport.SG:
• Personalisation - Quick access to your frequently used bus service and traffic information.
• My Notifications - Manage push notifications to receive MRT or Road alerts that interest you.
• Near You - View information on nearby bus services, MRT stations or traffic information in the vicinity.
• Multi-modal Journey Planner - Start planning your journey with different transport options based on your travel needs.

This application is a service of Singapore Government.
