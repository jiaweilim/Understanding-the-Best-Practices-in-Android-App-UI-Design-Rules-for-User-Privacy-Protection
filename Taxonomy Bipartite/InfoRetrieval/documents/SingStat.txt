Enjoy fast, free and easy access to Singapore official statistics and key ASEAN statistics with the SingStat Mobile App. View the data in chart and tabular formats spanning across 28 data categories, including GDP, Population, Prices, Labour, Manufacturing, Services and International Trade.

The app provides you with the latest information on Singapore's key economic and socio-demographic indicators at your fingertips. The app also allows you to bookmark your favourite indicators for quick access via My Bookmarks page, personalise the order of data categories, subscribe to push notifications to receive the latest data updates, perform quick analyses with the computation function and share charts and data tables via social media platforms.

For more information, please visit https://www.singstat.gov.sg/our-services-and-tools/singstat-mobile-app.

The mobile application is designed for devices with Android OS 4.4 and above.
